Band Bench Press

Target: Pectorals

Equipment: Band

Secondary Muscles: Triceps Shoulders

Instructions

  • Lie flat on a bench with your feet flat on the ground and your back pressed against the bench.
  • Grasp the band handles with an overhand grip, slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
  • Extend your arms fully, pushing the bands away from your chest.
  • Slowly lower the bands down to your chest, keeping your elbows at a 90-degree angle.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
